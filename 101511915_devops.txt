Learning DevOps is crucial for almost anything related to programming. The reason is that DevOps is widely used in various computer-related fields.
The course COMP 2156 offers a few projects to help students gain hands-on experience with DevOps tools that are important in the real world.

1. It provides hands-on experience with popular DevOps tools like Git, Jenkins, and Docker.

2. Students will also learn how to create and manage CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins.

3. Students will gain experience in deploying and managing applications in cloud environments like AWS or Azure.
