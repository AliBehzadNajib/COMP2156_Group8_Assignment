George Brown College is a college named after journalist and politician George Brown. The college offers over 200 programs to help students on the pathway to various careers.

1. George Brown College was founded in 1967 and named after journalist and politician George Brown.

2. The college has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.

3. The college is a nationally ranked research institution.

4. Their sports team is the George Brown Huskies.

5. George Brown has multiple libraries across its campuses.
